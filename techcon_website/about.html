History

TechCon began as a local meetup in 2010 and has grown into an international conference celebrating innovation in technology. Over the years we've showcased breakthrough talks, hands-on workshops, and published proceedings highlighting industry trends.

Mission

Our mission is to connect creative technologists, practitioners, and learners to build useful, accessible, and ethical technology. We run hands-on sessions and inclusive talks to advance practical knowledge and collaboration.
